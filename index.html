🔒 Overview

Coupley ("we", "our", "us") is a couples app built on intimacy and trust. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We believe your relationship is yours — we are only a tool to help you nurture it.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Coupley experience:

  • Account data — your Apple ID identifier (via Sign in with Apple) used to authenticate you. We do not store your email unless you choose to provide one.
  • Couple data — moods, reactions, messages, chat photos, goals, challenges, dreams, memories, anniversaries, and any content you create together.
  • Usage signals — mood history, love-language signals, and partner preferences that power AI suggestions within your couple.
  • Device info — locale and currency settings used to display prices in your region.
  • Purchase data — subscription status confirmed through Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment details.

💑 How Your Data Is Shared

Your couple data is visible only to you and your connected partner. No one else — including other Coupley users — can access your moods, messages, memories, or goals.

When you disconnect from a partner, your shared workspace remains tied to that pairing. A new partner cannot see data from a previous relationship.

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • AI suggestions are generated using only your couple's data and are not shared externally.

🤖 AI & Relationship Coach

The AI Relationship Coach uses your mood patterns, couple profile, love-language signals, and shared activity to generate personalised suggestions. This data is processed to serve you suggestions and is never used to train models shared with other users or third parties.

📸 Photos & Media

Photos you share in chat, memory entries, dream boards, and anniversary covers are stored securely and are accessible only within your couple. Premium features that include photos (Memory Capsules, Dream Board, anniversary covers) are deleted if you permanently delete your account.

🌍 Data Storage & Security

All data is stored with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We use secure cloud infrastructure and follow best practices to protect your information from unauthorised access.

🗑️ Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at Manhcuong531@gmail.com. Upon verified request, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all couple data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
